Description

From start to finish, this indulgent and classic spaghetti carbonara recipe takes just 30 minutes to make.

The ingredients are simpleâ€”just spaghetti (or another long pasta), and the carbonara is made with pancetta or bacon, eggs, Parmesan, a little olive oil, salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
